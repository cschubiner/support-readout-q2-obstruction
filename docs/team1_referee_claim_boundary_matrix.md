> If the supplied operator-algebraic, locally covariant, sector, recovery,

## Minimal External Classification Needed

- `known`: "This is theorem X; cite Y; novelty should be demoted to an
  application."
- `false`: "Row X fails under natural hypothesis Y; here is the counterexample."
- `missing hypothesis`: "The statement is true only after adding hypothesis X."
- `too broad`: "The comparison class is unphysical because standard axiom X is
  always assumed in the intended setting."
- `plausibly new`: "I do not know a standard theorem covering this exact
  support/readout/binary-rank boundary."

recorded in the outreach tracker.
